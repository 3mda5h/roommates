How to add an item to roommate shopping list

1. In the homescreen, click on the button titled "shopping list"
2. This will direct you to a page showing a list of items currently in the shopping list.
At the bottom of this list, click the "add item" button
3. A popup will appear. Enter the name of the item you wish to add.
4. Click the "add item" button at the bottom of the popup window
5. The item will appear in the shopping list, along with your username


How to remove items from the roommate shopping list

1. In the homescreen, click on the button titled "shopping list"
2. This will direct you to a page showing a list of items currently in the shopping list.
Ensure the list is not empty before trying to remove an item.
3. Select an item by clicking on the box to the left of the item name. A checkmark will appear in the box.
4. Click the "remove Items(s)" button at the bottom of the shopping list
5. A popup window will appear asking for confirmation.
If you are sure you want to delete the items, click the "remove item(s)" button
6. The item(s) you selected will be removed from the shopping list
